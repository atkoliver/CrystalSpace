### DuzySpace World Generator

## WHAT IS THIS???????

**DuzySpace** is a world-generator that makes space-themed server worlds filled with randomly-generated planets and asteroids. Easiely customizable with config files. The plugin is dependent on [Multiverse](https://mvplugins.org/core/) (or a similiar plugin) to actually generate a world with DuzySpace.

[! SPAAAAAAACE!!](README-image.jpeg)

History of Plugin:
* 2011: iffa spawns 'styxspace'
* 2011-2013 kitskub forks and rebrands plugin as 'bspace'
* 2013-2016: The CrystalCraft crew forks and rebrands plugin as 'DuzySpace' 
* 2026s: atkoliver forks and rebrands as 'DuzySpace' to 1.21.11. All gameplay stuff is cut out to focus on world-generation.
